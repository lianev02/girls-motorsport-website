The Stories Behind the Stats

Get to know the women changing motorsport from the inside out.

Meet them
Driver

Abbi Pulling rises through the ranks

From grassroots karting to F1 Academy frontrunner and Alpine development driver, Abbi represents a new generation of women breaking into top-tier racing.

Driver

Marta García makes history

Marta claimed the inaugural F1 Academy title in 2023, earning a fully-funded seat in FRECA and proving that consistent performance opens real doors.

Engineer

Ruth Buscombe's impact on race strategy

As one of the few female strategy engineers in F1, Ruth has worked with teams like Sauber and Haas, turning data into podiums and pushing for more diversity behind the scenes.

Engineer

Bernie Collins brings strategy to the screen

Formerly Aston Martin’s head of race strategy, Bernie now breaks down F1 tactics on live TV — proving that engineering expertise belongs in the spotlight.

Presenter

Niki Shields on the future of racing

As the face of Formula E, Niki champions electric racing and speaks about the tech, innovation, and sustainability driving motorsport forward.

Presenter

Laura Winter on and off the mic

A rising voice in F1 media, Laura brings depth, energy, and a clear commitment to growing the sport’s inclusivity—on screen and behind the scenes.
